Welcome to IFR 2021

With great pleasure, I welcome you to the first ever International Fleet Review (IFR) 2021 organized by Bangladesh Navy to commemorate the Golden Jubilee of the Independence of Bangladesh. IFR 2021 will be held in Cox’s Bazar, Bangladesh from 07-10 December 2021.

IFR 2021 will be an ideal opportunity to foster and strengthen existing relationships with all navies present being organized along the sea shore of one of the most beautiful places in Bangladesh. We extend you a very warm welcome and offer you to enjoy the solemn tranquillity of Cox’s Bazar, the longest unbroken sea beach of the world. In the present context, in order to have synergy in our maritime activities we seek out each other’s cooperation and support in rendering safety and promote safe passage in maritime domain....
